Chicken Chop

Description

Delicious grilled or pan-fried marinated chicken covered in a rich, bold black pepper sauce.

Top choice of the day if you are dead hungry.

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 1 piece Tyson® Chicken Thighs
  • 1/2 tsp Soy Sauce
  • 1/2 tsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 3/4 tsp Ketchup
  • 1/2 tsp Brown Sugar

Sauce

  • 1/4 tsp Soy Sauce
  • 1/4 tsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1/4 tsp Brown Sugar
  • 1/8 tsp Ground Black Pepper
  • Salt (as needed)
  • 1/4 tsp Corn Starch

Steps

  1. To make the marinade, mix Soy Sauce (1/2 tsp) , Worcestershire Sauce (1/2 tsp) , Ketchup (3/4 tsp) , and Brown Sugar (1/2 tsp) in a small bowl.
  2. Pour marinade over the Tyson® Chicken Thighs (1 piece) and gently massage it into the chicken.
  3. Let the chicken marinade in the fridge for at least 2 hours and up to overnight.
  4. Grill or pan-fry the chicken over medium-high heat for about 2 minutes on each side or until fully cooked. Set aside.
  5. In a small bowl, mix Soy Sauce (1/4 tsp) , Worcestershire Sauce (1/4 tsp) , Brown Sugar (1/4 tsp) , Ground Black Pepper (1/8 tsp) , Salt (as needed) , and Corn Starch (1/4 tsp).
  6. Melt Butter (3/4 tsp) in a pan over medium-low heat, then add Olive Oil (1/2 tsp) .
  7. Add Onion (1/8) and Garlic (1/2 clove) . Saute until the onions are soft and translucent.
  8. Add the black pepper sauce mixture, then turn up the heat. Let the sauce simmer for just a minute. This will allow it to thicken up.
  9. Pour the sauce over the chicken, then serve right away. Enjoy!
